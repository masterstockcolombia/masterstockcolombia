Privacy Policy

MasterStock collects and processes enterprise data strictly for transaction facilitation. We employ end-to-end encryption for all inventory manifests and buyer identities. Data is never shared with third parties without explicit consent, except as required by international trade law. User data retention is limited to the statutory period required for tax and audit compliance. By using this service, you consent to the cross-border transfer of data necessary for logistics execution.

Terms of Service

Access to the MasterStock platform is restricted to qualified B2B entities. All listings constitute binding offers. MasterStock reserves the right to suspend accounts for non-compliance with our grading standards or payment terms. Disputes are resolved via binding arbitration in the jurisdiction of the transaction origin. Users acknowledge that all liquidated assets are sold 'as-is' unless otherwise certified by a MasterStock-approved inspection facility. Unauthorized scraping of pricing data is strictly prohibited.

Escrow Agreement

Funds deposited for transactions are held in a segregated, non-interest-bearing escrow account with a Tier-1 financial institution until buyer verification of receipt. Release of funds is triggered upon 'Proof of Verification' (PoV). In the event of a dispute, funds remain frozen pending arbitration outcome. MasterStock acts solely as the escrow agent and bears no liability for the underlying asset condition beyond the certified grading report. Settlement currency is USD unless otherwise stipulated.
